BeDeft Tech

Geocalitzation in large events is complicated due to overcrowding. Finding your way or looking for your seat might be difficult. With BeDeft it can be solved. Using beacons technology, the product allows you to easily find your seat in big spaces. It is easy and user friendly. It is also adapted to people with limited mobility in order to help them to find the best path to their final place. The app contains different versions, depending on the type of show or sports event.
