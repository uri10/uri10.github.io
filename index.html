About

Feel your choice

As technology has moved forward, shaping the restoration sector, quick communication and feedback becomes a key point to stand out. Feel Your Choice is an innovative business focused on the development of tactile interactive surfaces. We have come up with a variety of costumizable solutions. We are able to fit in diferent types of businesses, from Casinos and Bingos to high - end restaurants. The implementation of the software is adequate for every market, facilitating, for example, leisure in Casinos.

BeDeft

Geocalitzation in large events is complicated due to overcrowding. Finding your way or looking for your seat might be difficult. With BeDeft it can be solved. Using beacons technology, the product allows you to easily find your seat in big spaces. It is easy and user friendly. The app contains different versions, depending on the type of show or sports event.
